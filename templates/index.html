Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the model predict age?

The model processes facial features and assigns an age category based on a deep learning algorithm trained on diverse datasets. It analyzes over 68 facial landmarks and compares patterns with known age groups.

What are the age bins used?

The model categorizes ages into bins of 5 years, such as 0-5, 5-10, 10-15, and so on up to 70+. This binning approach provides more reliable predictions than attempting to predict exact ages.

Can the model be improved?

Yes! Continuous training with new datasets can improve accuracy and adaptability to different facial structures. We regularly update our models with more diverse training data to enhance performance across various demographics.

How is user privacy protected?

We take privacy seriously. All image processing happens locally in your browser or on our secure servers with immediate deletion after processing. No facial images are stored or used for any purpose other than the immediate age and gender prediction.

What technologies power this system?

Our system uses a combination of OpenCV for face detection, custom-trained convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for feature extraction, and advanced classification algorithms for the final predictions. The backend is powered by Python with TensorFlow, while the frontend uses JavaScript and HTML5.
